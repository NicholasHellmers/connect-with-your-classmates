How to use the extension:

  • Go to the Canvas Course you wish to scan.
  • Make sure that you have loaded all of your peers to your computer by scrolling all of the way down or clicking the 'Load more peers' button.
  • If you clicked the 'Add connections' button prematurely, simply click it again to add the button to the missing rows.
  • In the newly created 'Actions' column, you will be able to see 'Connect' buttons on every row. Click it to connect with your peer!
