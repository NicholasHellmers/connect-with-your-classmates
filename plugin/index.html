Disclaimer:

This is the alpha version of the extension, so it is very buggy and with very limited features. Please, use it at your own risk.

How to use the extension:

  • - Go to the Canvas Course you wish to scan.
  • - Make sure that you have loaded all of your peers to your computer by scrolling all of the way down or clicking the 'Load more peers' button until you can't load any more users.
  • - If you clicked the 'Scan Users' button prematurely, simply click it again to add the button to the missing rows.
  • - In the newly created 'Actions' column, you will be able to see 'Connect' buttons on every row. Click it to connect with your peer!
